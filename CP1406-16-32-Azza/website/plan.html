Group Number - 32

Members: Aiden Low, Jacob Hoffensetz, Joshua Brown, Azariah Vaki

Goals

The goal of this website will be to increase the amount of ticket sales for each concert, and the sub-goal is to attract more sponsors for the BRO. The site is needed because the organisation struggles for money. This site will ultimately be designed to increase revenue for the organisation.

Success Evaluation

The success of the website can be determined by looking at ticket sales pre website and post website. Also by looking at the change in the amount of sponsors and donations after the website.

Target Audience

The target audience is people aged 15 to 35. To cater for this audience the website must be stylish and modern looking. The website must have clear navigation and a neat layout. The website will have a prestigeous look to attract the right audience. Generally the website will use the least amount of text as possible. Also, all links will be clear and easy to use.
